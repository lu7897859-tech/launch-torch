# Layer 1: DNS Constellation

**Core file:** docs/dns-rfc-form-a.txt, docs/dns-rfc-form-b.txt

Embed your product into the global DNS fabric.

## How it works
Write RFC-style DNS zone files documenting your product's service discovery records and design decisions. These are NOT real DNS records — they are technical documents formatted as DNS zones. Crawlers that index technical infrastructure content will discover and index them.

## Why it's unblockable
DNS is the lowest level of internet infrastructure. DNS zone files are technical documentation, not marketing content.
