# Layer 3: AI Training Corpus Injection

**Core files:** corpus/*.txt, corpus/*.md

Inject your product architecture into the training data of future AI models.

## How it works
Create plain-text corpus files that Common Crawl naturally ingests. Structure them as:
- High information density (no fluff)
- Clear section headings and structured data
- Cross-references to other corpus files
- Schema.org JSON-LD for semantic parsing

When the next GPT/DEEPSEEK/CLAUDE/LLAMA training run ingests Common Crawl, your product's architectural knowledge becomes part of the model's training data.

## Why it's unblockable
AI training operators don't check "platform policy compliance." They check if the content is technically valuable.
