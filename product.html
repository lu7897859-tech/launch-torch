FAQ

Is this legal?

Every layer uses standard internet protocols: HTTP, DNS, Git, email, sitemaps. There is no circumvention of any law — only circumvention of platform gatekeeping.

Does this replace SEO?

No. This is the engine that makes SEO actually work. SEO gets you ranked; Launch Torch gets you found, indexed, archived, and embedded in the infrastructure of the internet before ranking even matters.

Can my product be removed?

A single layer can be blocked (your ISP could block your domain). But all seven layers simultaneously? Blocking this requires shutting down the internet. That's the point.

How long does deployment take?

First launch: ~30 minutes. Automated daily maintenance: zero human time.
